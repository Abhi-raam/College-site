About Us

GOOD SHEPHERED COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY

Good Shepherd College of Engineering and Technology (GSCET) is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi & affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. This institution is run by “Good Shepherd Education Trust”. The Chairman of the Trust is Mr. G.D. Mitra, an educationalist with a Post Graduate Degree in Mathematics and a Degree in Education. Having experience of over 45 years in the field of education as a teacher, academician and administrator he has a passion for imparting quality education to all classes of the society, especially the less privileged younger generations mainly hailing from the rural areas. The Good Shepherd Education Trust was established in the year 1988 for serving the society. Having an experience in the field of school education for 32 years, the trust has made its remarkable tag in the field of education. The other trust members also possess Masters Degree in the field of Technical Education and are holding responsible positions in India and abroad. The Good Shepherd Matriculation Higher Secondary School situated in Marthandam was established in the year 1988 and is well reputed all over the state for excellence in education. Over the last 32 years of its existence it has excelled academically producing very good results and rank holders in the public examinations. The Chairman’s ardent love for education and devotion to learning inspired him to establish an institution of higher learning and the Good Shepherd College of Engineering and Technology is the realization of his long time vision. Hence, the long awaited dream comes true for the welfare of rural communities. The College offers world class education in Engineering and Technology and has well experienced, qualified staff members, state of art laboratories with all modern gadgets and well established learning centres with adequate volumes of Books and Journals. The College provides a wide variety of co-curricular and extra-curricular activities which give a head-start to students, developing their personality and imparting skills of decision-making and leadership. The Campus Placement Department assists the students to secure good jobs for a bright future. Good Shepherd College of Engineering and Technology encourages innovation and creativity amongst our students and our Faculty members. The college is providing holistic education, enabling students to stand on their own feet. The focus is always on the teaching- learning process developing the talents ensuring an all-round development amongst our students.

Principal

Dr. Bruce Banner

Dr. Bruce Banner, B.E, M.Tech, Ph.D., Principal of Good Shepherd College of Engineering and Technology. She obtained his Ph.D. degree in the Faculty of Information and Communication Engineering from Anna University, Chennai. She had working experience in reputed institutions. She has published 18 papers in peer-reviewed international journals and 2 papers in national journals. She has presented 10 papers both at national and international conferences. She has published six books and three patents. She has organized nine seminars/workshops, one hackathon, and two project expos. She has acted as Session Chair in three International and two National conferences. She is a member of the Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) and ACM. She has received the Best Faculty Award from South Asian Expo 2019, the Best Motivator Award, and the Achiever Award from Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education. She takes a role in encouraging, training, and motivating the students to get placed in reputed organizations.
